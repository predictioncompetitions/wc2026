Please read this before submitting. FIFA's official system for allocating the best third-placed teams to specific Round of 32 slots depends on which six groups the qualifiers come from — a combination that can't be known until the group stage ends. This competition can't replicate that system exactly at prediction time.

We have done our best to mirror the real fixture structure. Your top 8 third-placed teams still qualify based on your rankings, and safeguards prevent a group winner from facing the third-placed team from their own group in Round of 32. However, some specific match pairings may differ from the official FIFA bracket once the actual group stage concludes.

Knockout match scores are judged positionally — Team 1 vs Team 2 — so your score predictions will still be evaluated correctly regardless of which teams end up in those slots in reality. This only affects which teams you are asked to predict scores for in Round of 32.
