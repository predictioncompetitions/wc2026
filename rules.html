Rules & Scoring

Everything you need to know about how points are earned — and how a winner is decided.

The basics

  • Entry fee is €10. Every cent goes into the prize pot — nobody takes a cut.
  • Submit your entry before the deadline. Entries lock the moment the first match kicks off.
  • Predict every group, rank the 12 third-placed teams, then predict every knockout score from the Round of 32 to the Final.
  • No draws in knockout predictions. For a penalty win, record it as a 1-goal win (e.g. 2–1).
  • The third-place match is not included in the competition.
1
Group Stage
4 pts Correct team in the correct position (1st, 2nd or 3rd)
1 pt Correct team but in the wrong position
2 pts Correct group winner
5 pts Perfect group — all three positions exactly correct
2
Team Progression

Points are cumulative — they stack as your picked teams progress further.

3 pts Team reaches the Round of 32
3 pts Team reaches the Round of 16
7 pts Team reaches the Quarter-finals
10 pts Team reaches the Semi-finals
15 pts Team reaches the Final
20 pts Team wins the World Cup
3
Knockout Match Scores

Points below are awarded per match across all knockout rounds.

3 pts Exact score (e.g. you predict 2–1 and it finishes 2–1)
3 pts Correct winner
2 pts Correct number of goals for each team individually
1 pt Correct goal difference
Maximum per match: 10 pts
!
Important Rules

No draws are allowed in knockout predictions.

A penalty shootout win counts as a 1-goal win in the score.

Scores are applied to the fixture position, not the team — so if teams are listed home/away, your score prediction applies in that order.

The third-place play-off match is not included in the competition.

#1 Total points
#2 Progression points (total from team progression category)
#3 Number of exact score predictions
#4 Group stage points
