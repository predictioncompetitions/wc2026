Rules & Scoring

Everything you need to know about how points are earned — and how a winner is decided.

The basics

  • Entry fee is €10. Every cent goes into the prize pot — nobody takes a cut.
  • Entries are now closed. Every pick is locked in.
  • Predictions cover every group, the 12 third-placed team rankings, and every knockout score from the Round of 32 to the Final.
  • No draws in knockout predictions. For a penalty win, it counts as a 1-goal win (e.g. 2–1).
  • The third-place match is not included in the competition.

Points are cumulative — they stack as your picked teams progress further.

Progression points are awarded to any team in your knockout bracket that reaches the round in question.

Knockout match scores

Points below are awarded per match across all knockout rounds.

3 ptsCorrect winner
2 ptsCorrect number of goals for Team 1
2 ptsCorrect number of goals for Team 2
1 ptCorrect goal difference
2 ptsExact score bonus — awarded when all four components above are correct
Maximum per match: 10 pts (3+2+2+1+2)
!
Important rules

No draws are allowed in knockout predictions.

A penalty shootout win counts as a 1-goal win in the score.

Knockout match scores are graded against the actual fixture in that match slot, not against the teams you picked. Example: if you predict Brazil 2–1 Germany for match 73 and the actual match 73 is France 0–1 Spain, your prediction is scored as 2–1 vs 0–1 — so you'd score 2 pts for correctly predicting the away team's goal tally of 1, and nothing else.

The third-place play-off match is not included in the competition.
